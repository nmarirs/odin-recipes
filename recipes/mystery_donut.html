Mystery Donut


About the dish

To be a donut, or not to be a donut, that is the question

Well even we are not sure. It kinda just phases into existence and vanishes after you take a bite. Some say the donut will appear before you and your next of kin till the end of time.

Ingredients

  • A regular donut
  • A cardboard box
  • Radioactive isotope of your choice
  • Mr.Donut's special Mystery Donut glazeTM (avaiable only at select retailers)
  • Spare microwave
Did you know: We contemplated naming this Schrodinger's Donut, but it just isn't the same y'know.

Steps

  1. Unpack Mr.Donut's speical Mystery Donut glazeTM and microwave the bottle. Do note that the microwave will explode, take adequate precautions.
  2. Remove the bottle, safely,from the now unusable microwave and unscrew the cap.
  3. Apply the glaze on your donut. We recommend using the entire bottle.
  4. Place the donut in your cardboard box. Seal the box.
  5. Close the box and never open it ever again. Store it someplace safe so that it doesn't accidentally open.
  6. If everything worked right, you should be seeing a donut appear before you in a few min. Take a bite, and it will disappear!
  7. Note: The donut will NOT go away until you take a bite. But you could delay its appearance by hurling insults at it (the donut will question its existence). It also find a way to replenish itself, thank the enslaved volunteer scientists at Mr.Donut for that!
