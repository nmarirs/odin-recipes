Atomic Sandwich


About the dish

When life gives you Plutonium-239, make the Atomic Sandwich

The Atomic Sandwich was inspired by a trip to [INFORMATION REDACTED] where our chefs witnessed the testing of the [INFORMATION REDACTED]. Enjoy this sandwich as the Pu-239 decays with flavor into Uranium, Actinium, Bismuth, Francium and a plethora of other elements. Complete with our special Nuclear Fallout Cream and Mushroom Clouds, its safe to say that you will never need another sandwich after you try this one.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Freshly prepared Nuclear Fallout Cream
  • Polonium-239 sourced from your local nuclear weapons storage facility (Other isotopes work just fine but our chefs really like this one)
  • Cabbage
  • Tomatoes
  • Dried Mushroom Clouds
  • Radioactive water from your local nuclear power plant
  • Optional: White phosphorous
Tip: Keep an army of your own on standby if the defense authorities of your state happen to pay a visit

Steps

  1. Gently place 1 Dried Mushroom Cloud into a bowl of radioactive water. Leave it aside to swell to the size advertised on the package.
  2. Grill the bread to your liking
  3. Spread Nuclear Fallout Cream on both slices of bread
  4. Take a slice of bread and put 4-5 spheres of Pu-239 on it.
  5. Add some cabbage on top. This step is absolutely vital to the final taste of the sandwich
  6. Cut a few tomato slices and add them to your sandwich
  7. Close the sandwich
  8. Gently remove your Mushroom Cloud from the water and place it on top of the sandwich
  9. Optional: Gently remove a few pieces of phosphorous from storage. Dry them out and place them on top of the sandwich
  10. Voila! You are done. The Plutonium-239 has a half life of about 24,100 years so calculate your decay rate to get it finish eating it by then!
